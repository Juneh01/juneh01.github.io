Zeng Junhao (曾俊皓)

Biography

I am a master’s student at the Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences, affiliated with the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS). My current research interest lies in LLM hardware acceleration and system optimization, with a focus on efficient inference, GPU programming, AI infrastructure, and hardware-software co-design.

I received dual B.Eng. degrees in Electrical & Electronics Engineering from the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC) and the University of Glasgow in 2025 through a dual-degree programme. I was selected as an Outstanding Graduate of UESTC and received First-Class Honours from the University of Glasgow. During my undergraduate study, I worked on 6DoF pose estimation acceleration, edge LLM inference optimization, embedded systems, and digital signal processing, and received the Outstanding Winner award in the 2024 Mathematical Contest in Modeling / Interdisciplinary Contest in Modeling (MCM/ICM). I am currently an LLM Agent Intern at Apple in Beijing, focusing on practical LLM Agent applications, Harness workflow architecture, and deployment-oriented system studies.

Research Interests

  • Efficient LLM Inference: low-bit quantization, runtime optimization, memory-aware inference, T-MAC, llama.cpp, vLLM, and edge deployment.
  • Agent Memory: memory architectures for LLM agents, long-context management, task memory, and reliable long-horizon agent workflows.
  • GPU Architecture and Programming: CUDA kernels, Tensor Core programming, CUDA Graph, pipelining, memory hierarchy, and performance profiling.
  • Hardware-Software Co-design: accelerator-aware neural network design, model compression, activation-function hardware, and DNN scheduling.
  • 3D Vision and Pose Estimation: RGB-D 6DoF pose estimation, HiPose, lightweight visual models, and edge-side perception systems.
